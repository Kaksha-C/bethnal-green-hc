Our Services

Comprehensive NHS healthcare for you and your family

🩺

General Consultations

Face-to-face and telephone appointments with our GPs for any health concern. Same-day urgent slots available.

💉

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care for diabetes, asthma, COPD, hypertension, and other long-term conditions with regular reviews.

👶

Child Health & Immunisations

Routine childhood vaccinations, developmental checks, and paediatric consultations.

👩

Women's Health

Cervical screening (smear tests), contraception, family planning, and menopause support.

🧠

Mental Health Support

Assessment and management of anxiety, depression, and other mental health conditions. Referrals to talking therapies.

💉

Minor Surgery

Minor surgical procedures including removal of skin lesions, joint injections, and wound care.

✈

Travel Vaccinations

Travel health advice and vaccinations for those planning trips abroad. Book a travel consultation in advance.

💊

Prescriptions

Repeat prescriptions can be ordered via the NHS App, online, or at reception. Allow 48 hours for processing.

🩸

Blood Tests & Health Checks

Phlebotomy service, NHS health checks for 40-74 year olds, blood pressure monitoring, and ECGs.

🔊

Smoking Cessation

Support and advice to help you quit smoking, including nicotine replacement therapy and counselling.

🧑

Elderly & End-of-Life Care

Comprehensive care for older patients including home visits, care planning, and palliative support.

🏥

Physiotherapy

Self-refer to NHS physiotherapy for musculoskeletal conditions. No GP appointment needed.

Need a service not listed? Please contact reception on 020 7364 0800 and our team will advise you on the best course of action, including referrals to specialist services.
