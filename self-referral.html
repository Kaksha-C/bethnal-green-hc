Self-Referral Services

Access NHS services directly — no GP appointment needed

You don't always need to see your GP first. Many NHS services accept self-referrals, meaning you can contact them directly. This can save time and get you the help you need faster.

Available Self-Referral Services

🧠 NHS Talking Therapies (IAPT)

Free, confidential support for anxiety, depression, stress, phobias, OCD, and PTSD. Available for anyone aged 18+.

Refer Yourself

🏃 NHS Physiotherapy

Self-refer for musculoskeletal problems including back pain, joint pain, sports injuries, and post-surgical rehabilitation.

Refer Yourself

👁 NHS Eye Care

Visit any NHS optician directly for eye tests and treatment. Free for under 16s, over 60s, and those on certain benefits.

Find an Optician

🦷 NHS Podiatry / Foot Care

Self-refer to podiatry services for foot problems including diabetic foot care, nail problems, and biomechanical issues.

Refer Yourself

🔊 Stop Smoking Service

Free NHS support to help you quit smoking. Includes counselling, nicotine replacement therapy, and prescription medications.

Get Support

🍸 Drug & Alcohol Services

Confidential support for drug and alcohol issues. No judgement, no GP referral needed. Open to anyone.

Find Support

👶 Health Visiting (0-5 years)

Support for parents and carers of children under 5. Advice on feeding, sleeping, development, and postnatal wellbeing.

Contact Service

⚕ Sexual Health Services

Free and confidential sexual health clinics for STI testing, contraception, and emergency contraception. No GP referral needed.
