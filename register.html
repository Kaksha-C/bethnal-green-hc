Register as a New Patient

Join our practice — everyone is welcome

Who can register? Anyone living in the Bethnal Green and Tower Hamlets area can register with us. You do not need proof of address, immigration status, or an NHS number to register. Healthcare is free for everyone.

Patient Registration Form (GMS1)

Personal Details

If you know it. It's a 10-digit number found on previous prescriptions or letters from the NHS.

Contact Details

Medical History

Name and address of your previous GP, if applicable
Please list any ongoing conditions (e.g. diabetes, asthma)
List any medications you are currently taking

Preferences

What happens next? We will review your registration and contact you within 5 working days. You may be invited for a new patient health check. Your medical records will be transferred from your previous GP automatically.
