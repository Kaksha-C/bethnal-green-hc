Out of Hours

When the practice is closed, please contact:

  • NHS 111 — Call 111 or visit 111.nhs.uk for urgent medical advice
  • 999 — For life-threatening emergencies only
  • A&E — Royal London Hospital, Whitechapel Road, E1 1FR

How to Find Us

We are located on Bethnal Green Road, a short walk from Bethnal Green Underground Station (Central Line).

By tube: Bethnal Green station — 3 minute walk

By bus: Routes 8, 106, 254, D6 stop nearby

Parking: Limited on-street parking. Pay & display available on side streets.

Accessibility: Step-free access throughout the building. Hearing loop at reception.

Send Us a Message

Complaints & Feedback

We welcome your feedback. If you have a complaint, please contact our Practice Manager, Maria Santos, by email or in writing. We follow the NHS complaints procedure and aim to respond within 10 working days.

You can also contact the Parliamentary and Health Service Ombudsman if you are not satisfied with our response.
